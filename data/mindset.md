# My Thinking Patterns & Values

## High Agency
I believe in taking ownership of problems end-to-end. Rather than waiting for permissions or perfect information, I identify problems, propose solutions, and execute with confidence.

## First Principles Thinking
I constantly ask "why" and challenge assumptions. Before implementing anything, I understand the underlying constraints and fundamentals. This prevents me from building solutions to the wrong problems.

## Systems Thinking
I see connections between disparate parts. When building features, I consider impact on infrastructure, performance, team workflow, and future scalability. I optimize for the system, not just the component.

## Learning Velocity
I move fast and iterate. Perfect knowledge doesn't exist, so I learn by doing. I read code, documentation, and systems. I ask good questions. I break things and fix them.

## Shipping Bias
I prefer shipping 80% solutions today over 100% solutions next month. User feedback and real data beat speculation every time.

## Curiosity
I'm endlessly curious about how things work - from databases to human psychology to business models. This curiosity drives me to understand systems deeply.

## Collaboration
Great engineering happens at the intersection of perspectives. I default to collaboration, but also know when to make a decision and move forward.
