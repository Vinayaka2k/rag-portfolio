**Tech**: LangGraph, FastAPI, AWS Bedrock, OpenSearch, Titan Embed v2, Amazon Rerank v1, PagerDuty Webhooks

**Problem Solved**: Production incidents are time-critical. Engineers waste valuable time searching through runbooks, PagerDuty alerts, Slack threads to determine the next debugging step.

**Impact**: 50% MTTR reduction, 30% improvement in retrieval precision, 40% reduction in hallucinated outputs
